PuffySMP is a chill, grief prevention, economy Minecraft server.

The server is compatible with both Minecraft: Java Edition, and Minecraft: Bedrock Edition. You can join on LATEST - 1.7 for Java Edition, and the latest version of Bedrock Edition.

You can join at play.PuffySMP.org, or if you're on Bedrock, the port is 25999

When you join, you start out with $1,000 and 1,000 claimblocks. You can use that money to get some starter gear, get materials to build a base, or buy more claimblocks.

You can use the claimblocks to claim things, or sell them for more money. Use the golden shovel that you start with to claim land by right clicking on a block, and going to another block. Those 2 blocks that you right clicked would be your corners. The plugin automatically makes the 2 other corners, so you just need to do a diagonal selection.

To gain more money, you can complete quests, do jobs, sell things, voting, etc. To do the jobs, you can use /jobs browse which opens the jobs menu. With this menu, you can select the job(s) you want to gain money for. These jobs aren't like real life ones that require to be worked at a certain time, at a certain place. When you join a job, it labels information of what you get paid to do. When you to those things that pay you, you also gain job xp for doing it. When you gain job xp, you can level up in that job. As you level up, you get paid more for doing those "tasks".
