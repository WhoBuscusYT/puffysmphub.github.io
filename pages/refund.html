You will only be provided with 10 refunds, with requesting more than 10 refunds will result in a store ban.

We are fine with refunds for purchased items including ranks and merchandise. Other things like claimblocks or crate keys will not be refunded as that you will need to have that exact amount of claimblocks/keys that you bought.

If anything was purchased to you as a gift, you cannot be refunded for it, and the person that gifted you it will need to request a refund. If you get gifted something and they request a refund, you will be notified. in game.

If you want a refund, please contact us at support@puffysmp.org or via a misc ticket in the Discord.
