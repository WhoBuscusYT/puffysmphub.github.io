1. No Griefing
We get that you might be broke and want to loot or explode some bases for the fun of it to get resources, but that player's base is part of server history, what if you quit, and then a little later, someone comes and explodes a base that took you a month to make?
If your base gets griefed, report it to staff and we'll come look at it. Griefing will be a warn, and continuation of it will be a ban.

2. Do Not Use Cheats
Cheats include, fly hacks, autoclickers, key-weights, etc.
If you're caught using cheats, you'll be banned.
If you're wanting to use the Replay Mod, we're going to trust you to use it only for recording.
If you suspect a player/mod (client mod) to have a cheat of some sort, please report it to staff.

3. Do not beg
Don't ask for free stuff, as we have a community chest at spawn, and PWs have community chests (if the pw owner adds one).
Nothing major will happen if you beg, will start out with a verbal warning, and then could lead up to a ban.

4. No Promotions
Don't talk about other servers, and don't promote other servers.
Don't promote your YouTube, Twitch, etc. Only verified partners can promote that.
PW Promotions are allowed, but only promote one pw per person every 30 minutes, to keep chat not flooded
30 minutes rule also applies to verified partner stream/video promos.

5. Do not lag the server
Causing unnecessary lag on the server isn't needed, we will find out who's doing it if it happens. We can tell the difference between purposeful and other lag.
If you suspect a machine that you build could potentially cause server lag, make a ticket and a staff member will come look.

6. Have fun on the server!
We hope that you enjoy your time on the server, and you can vote for us if you want to!
